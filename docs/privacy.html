Privacy Policy

HolyMatch values your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information. HolyMatch uses Apple, Firebase, Google Maps, and RevenueCat services for authentication, analytics, purchases, subscriptions, and location features. Ads may be provided via Google Mobile Ads (AdMob).

Data We Collect

  • Account & Identifiers: Firebase User ID, email, profile information, device identifiers (for ads/analytics).
  • User Content: Photos, biography, preferences, chat messages, and other information you choose to share.
  • Location: Approximate or precise location to show nearby users and apply distance filters.
  • Purchases: Subscription status and transactions via Apple & RevenueCat.
  • Usage & Diagnostics: Basic app interactions, crash logs, and performance data to improve stability.

How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To match you with other users based on your preferences and location.
  • To enable chat and interaction features.
  • To provide subscription services (e.g. Premium, Boost).
  • To ensure safety, prevent fraud, and enforce community guidelines.
  • To send notifications and service-related messages.
  • To analyze and improve app performance.

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party providers:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, Firestore database, Storage, Messaging.
  • RevenueCat: In-app purchases and subscription management.
  • Apple / Google Sign-In: Authentication options.
  • Google Maps & Geocoding: Location services.

Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time inside the app under Profile → Settings → Delete Account. When you confirm deletion, your profile, photos, likes, matches, and messages are permanently removed from our servers within 30 days. Your subscription status is managed separately by Apple and can be canceled in your Apple ID settings.

User Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at privacy@holymatch.app. We respond within 30 days.

Location Data

We process your location to show nearby users and apply your distance preferences. You may disable location sharing in your device settings, but some discovery features may not work properly.

Tracking / Ads

We may request permission to track (AppTrackingTransparency) for ad personalization and measurement. You can deny this and continue to use the app. We do not sell personal data.

Age Requirement

HolyMatch is strictly for users 18 years or older. We do not knowingly collect or process data of children under 18. If you believe a minor is using the app, please contact us.

Security

All network communication uses HTTPS/TLS. We apply reasonable safeguards to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Cookies Policy

Last updated: 15 Sep 2025

This Cookies Policy explains how HolyMatch uses cookies and similar technologies in our iOS app and on our support website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us remember information about your visit and improve your experience. While the iOS app itself does not rely on traditional browser cookies, our support website and certain third-party services may use cookies or similar technologies.

2. How We Use Cookies

  • Authentication & Sessions: Keep you signed in and maintain secure sessions.
  • Preferences: Remember language and user settings.
  • Analytics: Understand how users interact with the app and site (aggregate, non-personal data).

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that rely on cookies or similar technologies, including:

  • Firebase (Google) – analytics, crash reporting, push messaging.
  • Google Analytics – aggregated site usage statistics.

These providers maintain their own privacy and cookie policies.

4. Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies in your device or browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some features of the app or support site.

5. Updates

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with a new “Last updated” date.

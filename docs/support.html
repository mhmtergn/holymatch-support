Contact Support

For any issue, email us at support@holymatch.app.

Please include (if possible):

  • Your Apple ID email (optional), device model, and iOS version
  • App version (see Profile → Settings)
  • Brief steps to reproduce the problem
  • Screenshots or a short screen recording

FAQ & Troubleshooting

Login problems

  • Password reset: On the login screen tap Forgot password.
  • Apple / Google sign-in: Make sure the same Apple/Google account is selected and you’re online.

Notifications

  • Open iOS Settings → Notifications → HolyMatch and allow notifications.
  • In-app: Profile → Notifications and turn on the toggles you want.

Location & Discovery

  • Enable location in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → HolyMatch.
  • Adjust distance / age filters in Profile → Preferences.

Subscriptions & Purchases

  • Manage/cancel in your Apple ID: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions.
  • Payments are handled by Apple. For billing issues, request a refund via reportaproblem.apple.com.

Delete Account

You can delete your account in-app: Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes your profile, photos, likes, matches, and messages from our servers. Subscription cancellation must be done in Apple ID settings.

Safety

Please be cautious when meeting people in real life. Do not share sensitive information. Report users who violate our rules.
